or 形式 (子集卷积):
$$\begin{aligned}T_{ij} = [i|j=i] = [j \in i]\end{aligned}$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1&1\\0&1
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$

and 形式 (超集卷积):
$$\begin{aligned}T_{ij} = [i\&j=i] = [i \in j]\end{aligned}$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1&0\\1&1
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$

xor 形式 ($T$与自身互为逆矩阵):
$$\begin{aligned}T_{ij} = (-1)^{parity(i\&j)}\end{aligned}$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1&1\\-1&1
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$
